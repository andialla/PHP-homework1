About Chinese Zodiac

The Chinese zodiac, which is also called "Earthly Branches", is one of the numerals with duodecimal system. It used to be utilized as indicates of time, the moon, directions and many other things in the ancient China and Eastern culture.The twelve units are represented as twelve animal signs.
They are;
1) Rat,
2) Ox,
3) Tiger,
4) Rabbit,
5) Dragon,
6) Snake,
7) Horse,
8) Sheep,
9) Monkey,
10) Rooster,
11) Dog,
12) Pig,
 in order which they appear in the cycle.
